One Step Closer...

Hello, everyone! My name is Fadhilla Dwi Prameswary Rayes. You can call me Della. I was born in Mataram, Lombok Island. Now, I live in West Sumbawa.

Since I was little, I really love reading and writing. One of my first literature that made me love to read is Bobo's Magazine. However, I didn't read it anymore due to my age, haha. Now, I am reading books. My favorite genres for fiction are thriller, historical-fiction, and drama. While, for non-fiction, I really love philosophy, self-improvement, bio-/auto-graphy, and psychology. In the future, I want to build a library full of my collections in my hometown. Due to the lack of literature resource in there, I will provide an open access library for society.

I was nearly attended at Indonesian Literature Major in University. However, I choose Biology because I need to explore nature deeply and learn anything that is not in the book. Thus, based on my major, I have an idea to realize in the future. I want to build a recycle paper factory so that trees are no longer the only one main resource for paper. Beside it, I want to be a writer and publish books. I don't wanna contribute to deforestation and global warming because of my books.

Yet, first of all, I have to get much money to make all my dreams come true. I have to get a job. So, I have track records during college that I think will help me to get jobs. Let us go to the below!
